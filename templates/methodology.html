Methodology

How the Digital Twin is built

The system collects responses, assigns predefined scores to each option, and translates them into a compact behavioral profile.

Step 1

Data capture

Users answer 25 exact questions across habits, values, risk, social behavior, and decision making.

Step 2

Weighted scoring

Radio options carry predefined scores, while 1–10 sliders are mapped directly onto behavioral dimensions.

Step 3

Dimension synthesis

The backend calculates introversion versus extroversion, risk appetite, logic orientation, and planning style.

Step 4

Prediction layer

The profile engine converts the scores into a personality summary and scenario-based recommendations.
