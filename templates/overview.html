Overview

What is a Digital Twin?

A Digital Twin of yourself is a structured behavioral mirror built from answers about habits, values, uncertainty tolerance, social preferences, and decision-making style.

Why it matters

A digital twin helps reveal patterns that are often hidden in day-to-day life. It can support self-awareness, reflection, mentoring, research presentations, and career exploration.

Where it can be used

Use it for personal introspection, behavioral analysis, academic demonstrations, and future-oriented decision modeling.

Capture habits, values, social patterns, and decision style with carefully designed questions.

Generate personality type, decision style, and predictive scenarios on the dashboard.
